📊 Iran War Oil 2026 – US Gas Tracker
📌 Overview

This project is a Power BI dashboard analyzing the impact of the 2026 Iran–US conflict on global oil supply and US gas prices.

It focuses on how geopolitical disruptions affect energy markets, particularly through critical routes like the Strait of Hormuz.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze global oil supply disruptions
Track gas price fluctuations in the US
Understand the impact of geopolitical events on energy markets
Convert raw data into meaningful insights

Tools & Technologies
Power BI
Excel
DAX

Dataset

The dataset includes:

Oil supply data
Gas price trends
Geopolitical event timelines

(Data sourced and structured manually for analysis)

📈 Key Insights
Disruptions in the Strait of Hormuz impact ~20% of global oil supply
Oil price volatility increases significantly during geopolitical tensions
US gas prices show direct correlation with global supply disruptions

📊 Dashboard Features
Interactive filters and slicers
Trend analysis of oil supply and pricing
Visual representation of crisis impact over time

🚀 Project Outcome

This project demonstrates how data analytics can be used to understand real-world geopolitical and economic scenarios through visualization and storytelling.
